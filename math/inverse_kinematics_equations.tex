\[\dot{\phi_R} = \frac{\theta}{r}(\frac{x}{\tan\theta} + y + \ell)\]
\[\dot{\phi_L} = \frac{\theta}{r}(\frac{x}{\tan\theta} + y - \ell)\]
\[r = \mbox{wheel radius}\]
\[\ell = \mbox{distance between wheel and robot center}\]
